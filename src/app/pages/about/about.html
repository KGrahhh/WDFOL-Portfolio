About Me

My career in technology development operates through two main forces which express my artistic skills and my logical reasoning abilities. I commit myself to learning new frameworks and design tools because I follow my passion for lifelong learning. I will develop myself into a Full-Stack Developer and UI/UX Designer who creates software solutions which turn creative ideas into concrete products through incremental coding work.
